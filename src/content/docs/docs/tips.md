Small things that make a big difference. Each tip links to the guide page
where it's covered in full.

## Capture faster

- **Scroll to grow your selection.** In the
  [unified overlay](/docs/guide/capture/#smart-capture-area--window), hover an
  element and scroll the wheel to step the highlight outward — button → panel
  → whole window — then click.
- **Freeze the screen to catch menus.**
  [Delayed capture](/docs/guide/capture/#delayed) freezes everything, so open
  dropdowns and hover states survive long enough to capture.
- **Skip the editor entirely.** Set the default destination to **Clipboard**
  in [Settings → Capture](/docs/guide/settings/#capture) and captures go
  straight to your clipboard to paste — no editor, no file.
- **Grab every display at once.** In the
  [fullscreen picker](/docs/guide/capture/#fullscreen), ⌘-click stitches all
  your monitors into one image.

## Edit like a pro

- **Set a tool's style once.** Each
  [annotation tool](/docs/guide/editor/#annotation-tools) remembers its own
  color, stroke, and opacity — your next arrow looks like your last.
- **Keep marks readable anywhere.** Add an **Outline** color so arrows and
  text stay visible over both light and dark screenshots.
- **⌘-scroll to zoom toward your cursor** — it works from
  [any tool](/docs/guide/editor/#zoom-and-navigation), no need to switch to
  the zoom control.
- **Undo survives everything.** ⌘Z reaches back across quits, capture
  switches, even deletes and metadata edits — one
  [global timeline](/docs/guide/editor/#undo-that-survives-relaunch).
- **Experiment without fear.** Duplicate a whole capture to try a different
  crop or annotation set, with the original untouched.

## Redact & protect

- **Let it scan automatically.** Turn on auto-scan in
  [Settings → On-Device AI](/docs/guide/redaction/#running-a-scan) and every
  capture is checked for secrets the moment it opens.
- **Solid fill, not blur, for real secrets.** Blur can hint at short strings;
  [Solid fill](/docs/guide/editor/#blur-and-redaction) is unrecoverable in the
  export.
- **You never miss a shot while locked.** With
  [Enhanced Security](/docs/guide/security/) on, capturing still works even
  when the library is locked — it just saves encrypted.

## Find anything

- **Search inside your screenshots.**
  [Search](/docs/guide/library/#search) reads the *text in the image* — an
  error message, an invoice number — not just filenames.
- **Space bar = instant preview.**
  [Quick Look](/docs/guide/library/#quick-look) any capture without opening
  the editor; ⌘-scroll resizes the preview.
- **Star what you return to.** Favorites and
  [collections](/docs/guide/library/#collections-and-favorites) keep the
  captures that matter one click away.

## Power moves

- **Drag straight out to Finder or Mail.** Drag a thumbnail from the
  [recent strip](/docs/guide/library/#the-recent-strip) to export a PNG with
  no save dialog.
- **Pause a recording from the keyboard.** **⌘⇧P** keeps your mouse out of the
  [recording](/docs/guide/recording/#while-recording) while you set up the
  next step.
- **Turn a table screenshot into data.**
  [Extract Structured Data](/docs/guide/ai/#extract-structured-data) pulls
  tables and fields out as copyable text.
- **Make a shortcut your own.** Rebind any command in
  [Settings → Shortcuts](/docs/guide/shortcuts/) — the recorder rejects a
  combo that's already taken.
