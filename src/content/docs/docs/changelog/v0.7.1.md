**Released July 16, 2026** ·
[Download](https://github.com/raydeng83/Sealshot-Release/releases/tag/v0.7.1)

## Recording

- The recording HUD now shows a Recording/Paused label beside the status icon.
- The record prompt now appears over full-screen apps, on the display being
  recorded.
- Each recording shortcut stops only its own recording mode.
- System and microphone audio are recorded at native 48 kHz for cleaner sound.
- A finished recording is selected automatically in the recent-captures strip.

## Editor

- One app-wide undo timeline: images, videos, metadata edits, and selection
  are all undoable steps, and each undo names what it undid.
- New Free Arrow tool — draw an arrow along any path, next to the straight
  Line Arrow.
- Pen, line, arrow, shape, step, and text annotations can now have an
  outline (casing) color with its own width control.
- Text tool overhaul: weight slider, per-tool color and opacity memory,
  visible text-box frame with resize dots, and typography remembered across
  launches.
- Captures render and export color-faithful — no more lightened text.
- Dragging a focused capture to Finder exports just the focus area.
- Duplicate a whole capture from the editor, the strip, or the Library.
- Extracted Data results appear in a single consolidated view; exports default
  to the image's name, and cancelling an extraction takes effect immediately.
- Structured Data Extraction uses the enhanced on-device model when it's
  downloaded.
- The toolbar and tabs freeze during blocking AI actions; navigating away
  cancels the action.
- Fit Width / Fit Height no longer leave a stray scrollbar.
- A remembered video reopens with its player panel and controls in place.

## Library

- Resize the Quick Look preview with the −/+ buttons, and grid tiles with
  ⌘+scroll.
- Play a video straight from its play badge in the strip; clicking the
  thumbnail opens it paused.
- Preview is available from the context menu, and the video action "Play" is
  now "Open".
- Drag multiple captures to Finder with a progress bar and Cancel.
- Empty Trash button clears the Trash in one click.
- Multi-select stays fast in large libraries.

## Security

- Idle auto-lock now follows system-wide idle time, not just activity inside
  the app.
- Menus and keyboard shortcuts are gated while the library is locked.
- Turning encryption on or off shows reliable progress and no longer damages
  video packages.
- Fixed a case where an interrupted recovery-key ceremony left the tab
  switcher disabled.

## Capture & OCR

- A cancelled capture no longer raises the editor over the app you were using.
- OCR now recognizes non-Latin scripts, with automatic language detection.
- Smart Redaction catches more: 2FA backup-code grids, role names, ticket
  IDs, masked card numbers, and street addresses.

## Other

- Deleted items are kept 7 days by default before being purged, and the
  retention setting now applies reliably to every trashed item.
- Your own tags are preserved when Smart Keywords are regenerated.
- Update dialogs use a cleaner centered-icon design matching the About panel.
