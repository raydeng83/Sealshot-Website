**Released June 18, 2026** ·
[Download](https://github.com/raydeng83/Sealshot-Release/releases/tag/v0.6.0)

Sealshot 0.6.0 made recording a first-class citizen and introduced encrypted
sharing.

## What's new since 0.5.1

- **Share packages** — export selected captures as an encrypted `.sealshare`
  package protected by a passcode (**⇧⌘E**), and open packages you receive
  (**⌘O**, drag and drop, or the File menu). See
  [Sharing](/docs/guide/sharing/).
- **Region and window recording** — record a chosen region or window through
  the capture overlay, not just the full screen, with a pre-roll countdown.
- **Recordings live in the Library** — recordings are stored alongside your
  captures, encrypted at rest with Enhanced Security, and play in an in-app
  player.
- **Floating recording HUD** — a draggable control to pause, resume, and stop
  an in-progress recording. See [Screen recording](/docs/guide/recording/).
- **Clearer microphone audio** — a new **Reduce microphone noise** option
  suppresses background noise and levels your voice, on device.
- **Menu bar rebuilt** — a persistent status item with full capture and
  recording controls, and each command's keyboard shortcut shown inline.
- **Security** — the Privacy & Security settings are gated behind Touch ID /
  password while Enhanced Security is on; view or regenerate your recovery
  code, replace the encryption key, and set an idle auto-lock.
- **Better scrolling capture** — smarter stitching and recovery on long,
  lazy-loading pages.
