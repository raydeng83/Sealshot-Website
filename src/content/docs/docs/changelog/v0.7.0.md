**Released July 9, 2026** ·
[Download](https://github.com/raydeng83/Sealshot-Release/releases/tag/v0.7.0)

Sealshot 0.7.0 is the latest direct-download release — signed, notarized, and
delivered automatically to existing installs via Sparkle. It's the biggest
release yet: on-device AI, a redaction-engine upgrade, collections, and
multi-monitor capture.

## Capture & recording

- **Multi-monitor capture** — with more than one display, fullscreen capture
  opens a monitor picker over a frozen screen; **⌘-click captures all
  displays** stitched together. Recording gets a per-display picker too.
- **Scrolling capture rebuilt** — more reliable stitching, and long browser
  pages no longer need any extra permission.
- Recordings are now **video `.seal` packages** — same secure container as
  images, with thumbnails, playback in the editor, and the same automatic
  metadata. See [Screen recording](/docs/guide/recording/).
- A smarter recording HUD that appears on the screen being recorded and can
  be pinned anywhere.

## Editor

- **Info panel** moved to the right sidebar — click to edit the name, summary,
  and tags; star a favorite; automatic **Smart Keywords** appear separately
  from your own tags.
- **Zoom, reworked** — ⌘-scroll zooms toward the cursor, a zoom field takes an
  exact percentage, and a new **Hand tool** pans.
- **Enhance Clarity** — tunable on-device upscaling and enhancement.
- **Remove Background** — on-device cutout with a transparent canvas or a
  background fill, fully undoable.
- **Crop tools** — cut or copy a cropped region (leaving a transparent hole),
  aspect-ratio lock, and a **Resize** popover for exact dimensions.
- **Richer text and styling** — a full font picker with rich text styling,
  smoother pen strokes, and per-object **drop shadows** with a position pad.
- **Live Text upgrades** — QR codes and barcodes are detected and clickable
  (open the URL or copy the payload).
- **One undo history** — resize, enhance, imports, metadata edits, and Remove
  Background all join ⌘Z, plus a **Revert to Original Image** command.
- Drag captures out of the strip or Library into other apps; drop files in to
  import.

## Smart Redaction

- **Enhanced on-device redaction** — an optional ~400 MB model (downloaded
  only with your consent; Apple Silicon) that recognizes sensitive content by
  meaning, not just patterns, with document-aware detection for financial
  documents and IDs (passport MRZ, SSNs, addresses, card details). See
  [Smart Redaction](/docs/guide/redaction/).
- **Smarter review defaults** — high-risk items (cards, keys, IDs, emails,
  phones, addresses) start checked; lower-confidence finds start unchecked.
  New **Select all / Deselect all** controls.
- **Thorough scan** — an optional Apple Intelligence backstop pass for items
  the detectors miss (macOS 26 on Apple Silicon).

## On-device AI

- **Extract Structured Data** — pull tables, form fields, contacts, and more
  out of a capture, then copy or export as plain text, Markdown, CSV, TSV, or
  JSON. See [On-device AI](/docs/guide/ai/).
- **Automatic titles, summaries, and Smart Keywords** for images *and* videos,
  generated on your Mac and editable in the Info panel.
- **Smarter search** — on Apple Intelligence Macs, search expands your query
  with related terms.
- Everything is controlled from the new **Settings → On-Device AI** tab, and
  it all runs locally.

## Library

- **Collections** — organize captures into collections from the sidebar, plus
  a pinned **Favorites** collection.
- **Quick Look** — press **Space** for a floating, resizable preview; works
  for videos too.
- **List view** — a sortable table with app, dimensions, size, date, and
  favorite columns.
- Filter by tag, by date, or by media type (images / videos), and resize grid
  tiles and the sidebar to taste.

## Sharing & export

- **Export to Package** — share as `.sealshare` (Sealshot) or `.zip` (any
  app), optionally encrypted with a generated passcode, with an expiry date,
  hint, and note. Export a whole collection or your favorites at once.
- **Export to Video** (`.mov`/`.mp4`) including batch export, and batch image
  export to a folder.

## Settings & shortcuts

- New **Capture**, **Recording**, and **On-Device AI** settings tabs, with
  per-tab Reset and a global Reset All.
- **Changed default: scrolling capture is now ⌘⇧W** (custom bindings carry
  over). New defaults: record screen **⌘⇧V**, record selection **⌘⇧R**,
  pause recording **⌘⇧P**, open Library **⌘⇧B**, new from clipboard
  **⌘⇧Q**, lock now **⌘⇧L**. The shortcut recorder now rejects combos
  already assigned to another action. See
  [Keyboard shortcuts](/docs/guide/shortcuts/).
