**Released June 14, 2026** ·
[Download](https://github.com/raydeng83/Sealshot-Release/releases/tag/v0.5.1)

A quick follow-up to 0.5.0 that laid the first foundations for screen
recording.

## What's new since 0.5.0

- **Screen recording (first version)** — record the full screen with system
  audio and microphone support, controlled from the menu bar. (Region and
  window recording arrived in 0.6.0.)
- **Library sorting** — sort your library by date, name, or size.
- **Broader Smart Redaction coverage** — new contextual detectors (names,
  addresses, labeled fields) plus more rules for financial data, network
  secrets, Social Security numbers, and `key=value` secrets.
- Editor quality-of-life fixes: new blank canvases auto-save, freshly
  captured images fit to the viewport, and undo/redo actions show a brief
  label of what they did.
